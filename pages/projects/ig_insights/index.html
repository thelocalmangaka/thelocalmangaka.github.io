IGInsights

This tool can calculate the total sum of likes, plays, watch-time, etc. on all your Instagram posts over their entire lifetimes!
This tool uses Facebook APIs and all you need to do is:

  • 1) Log into Facebook
  • 2) Create/associate a Facebook page with your Instagram profile (necessary to use Insight APIs)
  • 3) Log into Instagram
  • 4) Follow instructions to grant this tool permissions to view your insights.
    • I specifically request for the following permissions: [instagram_basic, instagram_manage_insights, pages_read_engagement, pages_show_list], as they are required by the Insights API here.
  • 5) Wait for page to finish calling Facebook's API's for all your posts, then display the results in a table in this window!

Results can also be downloaded as a .csv file!!!

This tool also only works for public Instagram profiles that are associated with a professional Business or Creator account.
...and so if that sounds like you, please enjoy this tool!
